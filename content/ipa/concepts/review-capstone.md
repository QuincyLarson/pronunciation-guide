title: Review capstone
summary: Capstones are less about new theory and more about proving that earlier symbols now work together under pressure.
---

By the time you reach a capstone, the main question is not "Have I seen this symbol before?" It is "Can I keep moving through a real word without freezing?"

Treat capstones as transfer drills. Slow down, decode what you know, and use the audio to confirm the parts that still feel unstable.
