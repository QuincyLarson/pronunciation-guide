title: Practical marks
summary: Small IPA marks are only worth learning when they change how you decode a real word on the site.
---

Not every tiny mark deserves a full theory lecture. This course only teaches the small marks that repeatedly help readers decode actual entries.

That includes things like palatalization, syllabic consonants, length, and tone-lite notation. The rule is simple: if the mark unlocks a pattern you will see on real pages, learn it. If it does not, skip the rabbit hole.
