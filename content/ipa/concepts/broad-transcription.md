title: Broad transcription
summary: The site teaches a practical dictionary-style notation, not the narrowest possible phonetic detail.
---

Broad transcription is a useful simplification. It captures the contrasts that help you read and pronounce a word without trying to encode every tiny articulatory detail.

That is why a broad English transcription may use one stable symbol set even though real speakers vary by accent, speed, and style. Learn the house symbols first. Fine-grained variation can come later.
