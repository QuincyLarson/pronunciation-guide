id: loanword-variants
title: Loanword variants
summary: Loanwords often keep some source-language features while dropping others, which is why multiple broad variants are common.
---

Words that move between languages rarely arrive as perfect phonetic copies. Speakers keep the features that fit comfortably and reshape the ones that do not.

That is why you may see two or three reasonable broad pronunciations for the same borrowed word. The course teaches you to read the variants as a map of adaptation, not as contradictory data.
