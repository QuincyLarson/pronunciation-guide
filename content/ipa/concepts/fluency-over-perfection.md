---
id: fluency-over-perfection
title: Fluency over perfection
summary: The goal is confident decoding and useful approximation, not paralysis while chasing an ideal accent.
---

Many learners stall because they want a perfect pronunciation before they can even read the transcription. That is backwards. Read first, refine second.

If you can move through the symbols, catch the stress, and produce a reasonable broad approximation, you are already succeeding. The audio and variants help you tighten the result over time.
