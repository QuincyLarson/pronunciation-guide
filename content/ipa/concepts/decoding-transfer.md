title: Decoding transfer
summary: The real win is using symbol knowledge on words you have never practiced before.
---

A course only pays off if it changes what happens on the next unfamiliar pronunciation page. That is decoding transfer: recognizing known symbol chunks inside a new word and staying calm long enough to assemble them.

Once you can do that, the directory becomes far more useful. You are no longer memorizing isolated entries. You are reading a system.
